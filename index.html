D3 Displays of Spark Data

Total Bytes Transferred to IP Over Time

This displays a time series of the time.json file, with a "scrub" at the bottom, so that the user can drag the bars and concentrate on a specific section.

Traffic by Byte Size

This displays a pie chart for the traffic_bytes.json file, in which the amount of traffic is grouped by byte size, to give a overview of the number of requests per size. The scale is logorithmic given the dispersion of the data.

You can click on different pieces of the pie to see a histogram of the request size breakdown per group
